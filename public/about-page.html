Why is the flu season so bad this year?

In this years flu season, about 7.5% of hospital patients have the flu. As a result of this, some pharmacies and offices have run out of the flu vaccine. Oddly enough, many older seemingly healthy people have caught the flu. It is estimated that approximately 84 kids and teenagers have died this year as a result of flu and its consequential problems. It is not clearly understood why this flu season has been so deadly.


Who is at risk?

According to the CDC, approximately 12,000 people die of the flu in a mild year. However, if the flu for the year is severe, up to 56,000 Americans could die. Furthermore, about 80% of the people are elderly. However, the flu can still target people who suffer from other complications such as heart disease. For this year, the most amount of hospitalizations occur for Americans aged between 50 and 64.



Is this years flu shot effective?

This years flu vaccine is most effective for children aged under 8. The flu vaccine typically works 51% of the time, according to the CDC. Despite this, the flu shot still is not effective in older aged people. However, even with the 25% overall effectiveness rate, it is still recommended to get the flu shot. These flu shots can be easily done at places such as local pharmacies.
